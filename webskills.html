UX;

UX views its customers from the outside in working closely with them to understand the promblems they face and helping to solve. ways to impact a customers experience based on research and engagement.

UX recipe for bringing project teams together include ;

-deep customer/user-engaged research
-close observation of your end users
-identify root causes
-develop a hypothesis based on your reseach
- test your hypothesis through prototyping and wireframes
- fail and try again
- improve on your failures
- test again
-fail again (perhaps)
-improve, test, fail, and continuing to repeat these steps
-execute, compete, and win
